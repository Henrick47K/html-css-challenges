TOP MOST POPULAR POKÉMON OF ALL TIME

Charizard

For many who started with Gen 1, Charizard was the first Pokemon they ever saw. This Fire type, draconic mascot has drawn fans in since the very beginning and never let go. Its striking design is spectacular, earning a major role in the anime and other media has helped it maintain its status as a Pokémon all-star. Its popularity has earned it not one, but two Mega Evolutions!Charizard often makes or tops many fans'favorites lists, and it isn't hard to see why.

Greninja

Greninja is a Water and Dark type, although its special ability allows it to take on the type of any move it uses. This, along with its intense speed and attack power has made it popular among competitive players. Taking the center stage during the anime's second renaissance, leading the charge to some truly jaw-droppingly animated fight-fests. whichever aspect you love, Greninja has probably managed to sneak onto your favorites list at some point.Can't beat that Water Shuriken.

Lucario

An unuual Fighting/Steel type, Lucario is a strong combatant, with great attack stats and the ability to Mega Evolve.But along with its abilites in battle, Lucario has a poweful "aura" of its own, with its frequent prominence in the games, as well as the anime lending it a devoted following.Basically, Lucario delivers a kind of effortiess cool factor that's almost unmatched in the franchise.

Gardevoir

First appearing in the Ruby and Sapphire games, Gardevoir is a wholly elegant humanoid with great fashion sense. Its beautful design and feminine appearence have earned it some of the most fan art of any Pokémon. It's xertainly loved in many different circles, that's for sure! Regardless of its undeniable aesthetic appeal, Gardevoir is a powerhouse, with a great Psychic/Fairy typing, excellent Special stats, and the ability to Mega evolve.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a monstrously powerful Psychic type, whose stats in the Pokémon franchise is quite literally legendary.Even decades after is creation,Mewtwo remains one of the strongest Pokémon.It's dark and tragic backstory have also given it a depth and mystique most other Pokémon don't have. Plus, its cool appearance makes its prominent battles across games, movies and ainime captivating to watch or participate in.
